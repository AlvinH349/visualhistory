Welcome to the Empire State Building!

The Empire State Building took only one year and 45 days to build! Construction started on March 17, 1930 and the building officially opened on May 1st, 1931.

The Empire State Building building was the tallest building in the US from 1931 to 1970. At 102-stories, the total height of the Empire State Building is 1,454 feet tall!

As of 2015, the building is America’s fifth-tallest skyscraper, and the 30th-tallest in the world. Between 10,000 to 20,000 people visit the building every day!
